Frequently asked

What is HourSpend?

An iOS app that converts every expense you log into the number of work hours it cost you. You set your hourly rate once; after that, every dollar has a duration. A $5 coffee isn't $5 — it's 23 minutes of your Tuesday.

Was this app called "Vantag"?

Yes. Same team, same app, new name. We renamed to HourSpend because the concept — spending hours of your life — deserved a name that says so out loud. Your account, data, and subscription continue exactly as before.

Is HourSpend free?

Core expense tracking, the hour-conversion engine, and the calculator are free forever. Premium unlocks AI chat (Vanti), unlimited custom categories, subscription alerts, advanced analytics, and widgets with no logo.

Does HourSpend connect to my bank?

No. Every expense is entered by you — by hand, by voice, or by scanning a receipt. We don't read transactions, we don't pull feeds. This is a choice, not a limitation: awareness only sticks when you're the one doing the logging.

Who is Vanti?

Vanti is the in-app AI assistant — a small gold pocket watch with opinions. Ask her "can I afford this?" and she'll answer in hours, not spreadsheets. She notices patterns gently and never lectures.

How do I delete my account?

In the app: Settings → Account → Delete account. Deletion is permanent — we erase everything within 30 days, except what we're required to retain for tax records (subscription purchases, up to 7 years). See our privacy policy for detail.

I was charged for something I didn't want.

Subscription refunds on iOS go through Apple, not us — we don't see your payment details. Open the App Store, tap your profile, go to Purchase History, find the charge, and use "Report a Problem." If Apple declines and you believe it's wrong, email us and we'll see what we can do on our side.

I'd like to change the currency or language.
  • Currency: Settings → Preferences → Currency. Live exchange rates update daily.
  • Language: Settings → Preferences → Language. We support 14 languages at 100% coverage.
My data didn't sync between devices.

Syncing uses your account, not iCloud. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account on both devices. Pull-to-refresh on the dashboard forces a sync. If it still doesn't work, email us with your account email — we can see server-side.

Feature requests?

We read all of them. The short version of our roadmap: voice-driven logging, shared goals with a partner, a watch complication, and a home screen widget with Vanti. If yours isn't on that list, tell us.

Known issues

iOS 26 — large text sizes cut off expense amounts

Fix in the next update. Temporary workaround: Settings → Display & Brightness → Text Size → slide back one notch.

Apple Watch complication doesn't update

Complications refresh on a schedule Apple controls. If yours is stale more than an hour, tap-and-hold the watch face → Edit → re-select the complication. A proper fix is coming.
