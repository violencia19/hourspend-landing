Questions

Things people ask us.

Is it really free?
Core features are free forever. Pro unlocks AI parsing, unlimited goals, and advanced charts for ~30 minutes of your life per month.
Do you sell my data?
Never. Your data lives on-device and in your personal Firebase instance. We don't sell, trade, or share. No ads ever.
How accurate is the hour conversion?
As accurate as the wage you enter. We use 160 work-hours per month (40hr/wk × 4.33).
Android?
iOS-only for now. Built native on iOS 17+ with Liquid Glass for the best possible experience before expanding.
What makes this different from Mint / YNAB / Copilot?
They show dollars. We show your life. Once you see a $35 lunch as 1.4 hrs of work, you can't unsee it.
Which languages are supported?
14 languages full native: English, Turkish, Spanish, German, French, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Italian, Dutch, Polish, Russian, Arabic.
